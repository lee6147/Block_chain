⚠ Cautions & Troubleshooting

Common errors and their solutions, listed in order.

▶

1. MetaMask doesn't work when opening the HTML file directly

Symptom

When you double-click index.html, the URL starts with file:///C:/... and MetaMask shows as "not detected".

Cause

For security reasons, MetaMask does not inject window.ethereum on the file:// protocol. You must access it via http:// or https://.

Solution

Double-click start.bat to run a local server (http://localhost:5500).

▶

2. "MetaMask was not detected" message

Symptom

Even when accessing via localhost, "MetaMask was not detected" is displayed.

Cause

The MetaMask extension may be disabled or locked (password not entered), which prevents window.ethereum from being injected.

Solution (3 steps)

  1. Check that MetaMask is enabled at chrome://extensions
  2. Click the 🦊 icon in the browser toolbar to unlock MetaMask (enter password)
  3. Refresh the page (F5) or click the "Retry" button
▶

3. Nothing happens after clicking "Connect Wallet"

Symptom

After clicking "Connect Wallet", the MetaMask popup doesn't appear, or it gets stuck at "Connecting...".

Cause

A previous connection popup may still be pending. MetaMask processes only one connection request at a time, so new requests are ignored until the previous one is handled.

Solution

Click the 🦊 MetaMask icon in the browser toolbar to see the pending popup. Choose "Connect" or "Reject" from there.

▶

4. "Please switch to the Sepolia testnet" warning

Symptom

Wallet connection succeeded but a yellow warning banner is displayed.

Cause

MetaMask is currently connected to a different network such as Ethereum Mainnet. This app is designed to work on the Sepolia testnet (Chain ID: 11155111).

Solution

Click the "Switch to Sepolia" button next to the warning banner to automatically switch the network.

▶

5. Unable to fetch price data

Symptom

"Unable to fetch price data" error is displayed instead of the price table.

Cause

Unstable internet connection, or you may have hit CoinGecko API's free rate limit (10-30 requests per minute). CORS policy may also block direct calls.

Solution

Check your internet connection and click the "Retry" button. The app will automatically try 3 fallback routes (CORS proxies) sequentially.

Week 2 Key Concepts

▶

EOA vs Smart Contract Account

EOA (Externally Owned Account): A regular wallet controlled by a private key. This is what MetaMask creates.

CA (Contract Account): An account controlled by code. Created when a smart contract is deployed.

Analogy

EOA = A personal bank account (withdraw with your signature). CA = An automated rules account (executes automatically when conditions are met).

Technical Detail

EOAs are controlled by ECDSA (secp256k1) private-public key pairs. CAs store EVM bytecode and their code only executes when called by message calls from other accounts.

▶

Seed Phrase

A master key consisting of 12-24 English words. With this alone, you can fully recover your wallet.

Never share it with anyone — seed phrase = ownership of all your assets.

Analogy

Seed phrase = Master key to a safe. If you copy and give it to someone, they can open your safe.

Technical Detail

A mnemonic word list generated from 128-256 bit entropy per the BIP-39 standard. It serves as a master seed that can derive infinite private keys via BIP-32/44 derivation paths.

▶

Sepolia Testnet

A practice network that works identically to the Ethereum mainnet, but ETH is free.

Chain ID: 11155111 (0xaa36a7)

Analogy

Testnet = Board game money. The rules are the same as real ones, but there's no actual value. You won't lose money even if you make mistakes.

Technical Detail

Sepolia is Ethereum's PoS (Proof of Stake) based test network. Identified by EIP-155 Chain ID (11155111), it prevents replay attacks during transaction signing.

💡 Beginner's Getting Started Guide

Practical tips for first-time users

1 How much should I start with?

The testnet (Sepolia) is free. No real money involved!

How to get test ETH

  1. Go to Google Cloud Faucet
  2. Paste your MetaMask wallet address
  3. Click "Receive" → Get 0.05 Sepolia ETH for free

0.05 ETH is enough to run dozens of test transactions.

2 Which coins should I look at first?

🟠 Bitcoin (BTC)

#1 market cap, known as "digital gold". The most stable and longest-running cryptocurrency.

Beginner recommendation: ★★★★★

🔷 Ethereum (ETH)

#2 market cap, the pioneer of smart contracts. Foundation for DeFi, NFTs, and more.

Beginner recommendation: ★★★★★

Understanding just BTC and ETH is enough to start. Learn about altcoins after you understand these two.

3 MetaMask Initial Setup Steps

  1. Install — Install the Chrome extension from metamask.io
  2. Create wallet — Select "Create a new wallet" → Set a password
  3. Backup seed phrase — Write down the 12 words on paper and store in a safe place
  4. Enable testnet — Settings > Advanced > Turn on "Show test networks"
  5. Select Sepolia — Choose "Sepolia test network" from the network dropdown
  6. Get test ETH — Receive free ETH from a faucet

4 Critical Warnings

🚫 Never share your seed phrase (12 words) with anyone — whoever has your seed phrase can take all your assets.

🚫 Anyone asking for your seed phrase via DM is 100% a scam.

🚫 Don't put real money on mainnet right away — practice on testnet first.

🚫 Don't believe "it will definitely go up" — cryptocurrency is extremely volatile.

5 How to Use This App

  1. Complete the MetaMask setup above
  2. Click "Connect Wallet" and approve in the MetaMask popup
  3. Verify that wallet address, balance, and network info are displayed
  4. If not on Sepolia, click the "Switch to Sepolia" button
  5. Check BTC and ETH prices in real-time in the price table below
  6. Expand the key concept cards to learn blockchain basics

Data source: CoinGecko API
